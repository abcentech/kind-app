# The gDx Production System

A repeatable workflow that turns **one monthly theme** into a complete discipleship
resource — teaching Scripture deeply while producing multiple content formats for
different platforms.

**Mission:** Every devotional helps believers *know Christ, grow in Christ, live for
Christ, and finish well.*

Every monthly series produces **five layers** of content.

---

## Layer 1 — The Monthly Journey

Begin with one compelling theme, then build a *journey* rather than isolated teachings.
The audience should feel they are travelling somewhere.

**Theme examples:** Secrets of Longevity · Faith in His Name · Walking in Wisdom · The
Fear of the Lord · Revelation of Christ.

Every episode answers one step of the journey. For example:

> God Gives Life → God Strengthens the Spirit → God Renews the Mind →
> God Teaches Stewardship → God Helps Us Finish Well

---

## Layer 2 — The Weekly Framework

Every week follows one memorable pattern — memorable enough that a child can repeat it.

**Examples:**

| Pattern A   | Pattern B    |
|-------------|--------------|
| Foundation  | Believe      |
| Faith       | Become       |
| Focus       | Build        |
| Fitness     | Bless        |
| Finishing   | Be Faithful  |

---

## Layer 3 — Daily Episode Structure

Every devotional follows **exactly the same structure**. (Full template and formatting
rules live in [style-guide.md](style-guide.md).)

1. **Opening Hook** — 30–60s. "What if…" / "Did you know…" / "Imagine…" / "Have you ever wondered…"
2. **Theme Scripture** — one primary passage; two maximum.
3. **Big Idea** — one sentence everything revolves around.
4. **Teaching** — 3–5 points, each with biblical explanation + supporting Scripture + practical application.
5. **Biblical Example** — at least one; prefer Bible characters over abstraction.
6. **Illustration** — memorable, drawn from nature, family, business, sports, history, science, agriculture, construction, or technology.
7. **Application** — "What should I do today?" Always practical.
8. **Prayer** — simple, Christ-centred, faith-filled.
9. **Weekly Challenge** — one action to practise.

---

## Layer 4 — Companion Short

Every devotional automatically produces **one separate Short** (30–60s). It is *not* a
summary — it is a fresh hook built around one truth.

```
Hook       Unexpected fact · question · statistic · observation
Scripture  One verse
Truth      One memorable lesson
Challenge  One action today
```

**Example** — Full teaching: *Life Is Authored by God* → Short: *"You only have 36,500
days if you live to be 100. Who gave you today?"*

---

## Layer 5 — Multiplication

Never create content only once. One episode becomes:

- One YouTube teaching video
- One YouTube Short
- One written devotional
- One podcast episode
- One email newsletter
- One social-media carousel
- One quote graphic
- One discussion guide
- One family devotional
- One prayer focus

---

## The gDx Teaching Formula

Every lesson moves through these stages — the pattern should become *invisible* to the
audience but *consistent* across every devotional:

> **Hook → Scripture → Revelation → Illustration → Application → Prayer → Challenge → Short**

## The Guiding Philosophy

Every devotional answers four questions:

1. **What does God say?** *(Scripture)*
2. **What does it mean?** *(Interpretation)*
3. **What does it look like?** *(Illustration)*
4. **What must I do?** *(Application)*

Follow this system every month and you build a recognizable teaching ministry where
people know exactly what to expect: **biblically rich, visually memorable, deeply
practical, and always centered on Jesus Christ.** Over time, that consistency becomes
the ministry's identity.
