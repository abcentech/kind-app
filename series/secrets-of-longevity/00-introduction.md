# INTRODUCTION
## The Gift of Long Life

> **Series:** Secrets of Longevity — *Seven Decades of Serving God*

### Theme Scripture
> **"With long life will I satisfy him, and shew him my salvation."**
> — Psalm 91:16 (KJV)

---

### The Opener

What if the secret to a long life wasn't found in medicine...

or money...

or even genetics...

but in a promise from God?

What if living well is not measured by how many years you have, but by how faithfully you
walk with God through those years?

> **"With long life will I satisfy him, and shew him my salvation." — Psalm 91:16**

Think about this for a moment.

How rich are you?

Not in money.

Not in houses.

Not in possessions.

How rich are you in **days**?

Even if you lived to be one hundred years old, you would have only **36,500 days**.

That's just **876,000 hours**.

No one leaves this world with a million hours in the bank.

A hundred years sounds like a long time — until you realize how quickly those days
disappear.

Time is the one currency God places into your hands every morning. Once it is spent, you
can never earn it back.

---

### The Question

That raises an important question.

Why do some people die in childhood...

while others live into old age?

Abraham lived **175 years**.

Isaac lived **180 years**.

Jacob lived **147 years**.

Was it merely genetics?

Was it their environment?

Or has God revealed timeless principles that lead to a long, fruitful, and satisfying
life?

---

### God's Vision

Listen to God's vision for His people.

> **"For as the days of a tree are the days of my people..." — Isaiah 65:22**

A healthy tree is deeply rooted.

It stands through storms.

It continues to grow.

It keeps bearing fruit year after year.

That is God's picture of your life.

Not a life cut short.

Not a life merely existing.

But a life that is **rooted, fruitful, enduring, and finished well.**

---

### What This Series Is

This series is not simply about adding years to your life.

It is about adding life to your years...

purpose to every season...

and faithfulness to every decade.

Welcome to **Secrets of Longevity: Seven Decades of Serving God.**

Over the next five weeks, we will discover from Scripture how God transforms the whole
person — spirit, soul, and body — so that we may serve Him through every decade and
finish our race with joy.

Because God's desire is not merely that you live long...

**but that you live long for Him.**

---

### Prayer
