# SECRETS OF LONGEVITY
## Seven Decades of Serving God

### Theme Scripture
> **"With long life will I satisfy him, and shew him my salvation."**
> **— Psalm 91:16 (KJV)**

---

## The Big Idea of the Series

God's promise is not simply that you will have many birthdays. His promise is that you
will walk with Him, strengthen your spirit, renew your mind, steward your body, serve Him
through every season, and one day declare with the Apostle Paul:

> **"I have fought a good fight, I have finished my course, I have kept the faith."**
> *(2 Timothy 4:7)*

That is the true **Secret of Longevity** — not merely living longer, but **living every
decade for the glory of Jesus Christ.**

---

## The Five F's of Longevity

| Week | F | Theme | Focus |
|------|---|-------|-------|
| 1 | **FOUNDATION** | Life Begins with God | Life is God's gift before it is our responsibility |
| 2 | **FAITH** | Strengthening the Spirit | A strong spirit sustains a long, fruitful life |
| 3 | **FOCUS** | Renewing the Mind | A renewed mind preserves a fruitful life |
| 4 | **FITNESS** | Stewarding the Body | Your body is God's temple — steward it well |
| 5 | **FINISHING** | Leaving a Legacy | God's goal is not just long life, but finishing well |

```
FOUNDATION → FAITH → FOCUS → FITNESS → FINISHING
   Life      Spirit    Mind     Body      Legacy
```
