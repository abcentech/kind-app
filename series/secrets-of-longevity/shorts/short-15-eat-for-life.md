# SHORT 15 — Eat for Life
> Companion to Episode 15 · *Secrets of Longevity in 60 Seconds* · 30–60s

**Opening:** "Welcome to **Secrets of Longevity in 60 Seconds**."

**🎯 Hook:** Did you know the very first test in the Bible was about *food*?

**📖 Scripture:** "Daniel purposed in his heart that he would not defile himself with the
portion of the king's meat." — Daniel 1:8

**💡 Truth:** Daniel turned down the king's rich food, asked for vegetables and water, and
ended up healthier than everyone else. He served kings for seventy years. His long life
started with one decision at the table. What you eat is stewardship — and discipline is
worship.

**🚀 Challenge:** At your next meal, eat for the race, not just the moment. Choose enough —
and thank God for it.
