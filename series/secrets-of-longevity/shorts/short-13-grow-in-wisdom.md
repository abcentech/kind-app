# SHORT 13 — Grow in Wisdom
> Companion to Episode 13 · *Secrets of Longevity in 60 Seconds* · 30–60s

**Opening:** "Welcome to **Secrets of Longevity in 60 Seconds**."

**🎯 Hook:** A man can have a head full of facts and still ruin his life. Another knows far
less and lives full of peace. The difference isn't *what they know*.

**📖 Scripture:** "Length of days is in her right hand." — Proverbs 3:16

**💡 Truth:** Knowledge informs you. Understanding explains things. But wisdom *applies* them —
it gets you across the river alive. Solomon asked God for wisdom, and length of days came
along in her right hand. And God gives wisdom to anyone who asks.

**🚀 Challenge:** Before your next decision, stop and ask God plainly: *"Lord, what is the wise
thing to do?"*
