# SHORT 10 — Speak Life
> Companion to Episode 10 · *Secrets of Longevity in 60 Seconds* · 30–60s

**Opening:** "Welcome to **Secrets of Longevity in 60 Seconds**."

**🎯 Hook:** Right now, in your mouth, you carry both death and life — at full power. Which
one have you been handing out?

**📖 Scripture:** "Death and life are in the power of the tongue." — Proverbs 18:21

**💡 Truth:** God built the world with words. Ten spies spoke death and died in the
wilderness. Caleb spoke life and walked into the promise. Your words are seeds — you'll eat
the harvest of whatever your mouth plants.

**🚀 Challenge:** Catch your next negative word. Stop it — and speak a promise of God out
loud instead.

---
**Quote graphic:** "If you don't like your harvest, change the seed in your mouth."
