# SHORT 01 — Life Is Authored by God
> Companion to Episode 1 · *Secrets of Longevity in 60 Seconds* · 30–60s

**Opening:** "Welcome to **Secrets of Longevity in 60 Seconds**."

**🎯 Hook:** You only have 36,500 days if you live to be 100. Who gave you *today*?

**📖 Scripture:** "The LORD God... breathed into his nostrils the breath of life." —
Genesis 2:7

**💡 Truth:** Before you ever took your first breath, God breathed life into humanity.
Your greatest possession isn't your money. It's *today*. Don't waste the day God gave you.

**🚀 Challenge:** Today, don't ask, *"How much time do I have?"* Ask, *"Lord, how do You
want me to use today?"*
