# SHORT 16 — Rest for Life
> Companion to Episode 16 · *Secrets of Longevity in 60 Seconds* · 30–60s

**Opening:** "Welcome to **Secrets of Longevity in 60 Seconds**."

**🎯 Hook:** You'll spend about twenty-five years of your life asleep. What if that's not
wasted time — but a gift?

**📖 Scripture:** "He giveth his beloved sleep." — Psalm 127:2

**💡 Truth:** God rested on the seventh day. Jesus slept through a storm. When Elijah was
worn out, God's first gift was sleep. Refusing rest says, "It all depends on me." Rest
says, "God is still working while I sleep." Rest isn't laziness — it's trust.

**🚀 Challenge:** Tonight, go to bed earlier. Hand your worries to God, and let your rest be
an act of faith.

**Quote graphic:** "Rest is the body's confession that God is in control."
