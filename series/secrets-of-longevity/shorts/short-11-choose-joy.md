# SHORT 11 — Choose Joy
> Companion to Episode 11 · *Secrets of Longevity in 60 Seconds* · 30–60s

**Opening:** "Welcome to **Secrets of Longevity in 60 Seconds**."

**🎯 Hook:** There's a medicine that costs nothing, needs no prescription, and you already
carry it inside you. The Bible named it three thousand years before the first hospital.

**📖 Scripture:** "A merry heart doeth good like a medicine." — Proverbs 17:22

**💡 Truth:** Joy isn't a reward for an easy life — it's a choice that strengthens you to
live a long one. Paul and Silas sang at midnight, in chains, and the prison shook. Don't
wait for the chains to fall. Choose joy inside them.

**🚀 Challenge:** Before you voice one complaint today, name three things God has done for
you — out loud.
