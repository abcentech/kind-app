# SHORT 09 — Think Life
> Companion to Episode 9 · *Secrets of Longevity in 60 Seconds* · 30–60s

**Opening:** "Welcome to **Secrets of Longevity in 60 Seconds**."

**🎯 Hook:** The longest conversation of your life is the one inside your own head — and it
never stops. What are you telling yourself all day?

**📖 Scripture:** "Be ye transformed by the renewing of your mind." — Romans 12:2

**💡 Truth:** Ten spies saw giants and thought death. Caleb saw the same giants and thought
life — and at eighty-five he was still strong. Same facts. Different minds. Your thoughts
today become your life tomorrow.

**🚀 Challenge:** Catch your next fearful thought. Say it out loud — then answer it out loud
with what God says.

---
**Quote graphic:** "Where you fix your mind, there you will live."
