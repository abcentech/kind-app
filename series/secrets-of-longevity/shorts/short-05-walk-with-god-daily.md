# SHORT 05 — Walk with God Daily
> Companion to Episode 5 · *Secrets of Longevity in 60 Seconds* · 30–60s

**Opening:** "Welcome to **Secrets of Longevity in 60 Seconds**."

**🎯 Hook:** Genesis 5 says "and he died" eight times. Then one man breaks the pattern — and never has a funeral.

**📖 Scripture:** "And Enoch walked with God: and he was not; for God took him." — Genesis 5:24

**💡 Truth:** Enoch walked with God for three hundred years. Not one mountaintop moment — a daily walk. Nobody drifts into closeness with God. You step into it. One ordinary day at a time.

**🚀 Challenge:** Take one step with God today. Read a little, pray a little, obey what you hear. Then do it again tomorrow.
