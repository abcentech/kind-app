# SHORT 08 — Serve God Through Every Season
> Companion to Episode 8 · *Secrets of Longevity in 60 Seconds* · 30–60s

**Opening:** "Welcome to **Secrets of Longevity in 60 Seconds**."

**🎯 Hook:** We treat life like a stadium — a short game in the middle, and everything else is just the stands. God doesn't run a stadium.

**📖 Scripture:** "They shall still bring forth fruit in old age; they shall be fat and flourishing." — Psalm 92:14

**💡 Truth:** Samuel served as a child. David faced Goliath as a youth. Anna worshipped into her eighties. There are no spectator seasons in God's kingdom. Every decade has a divine assignment — and not one of them is wasted.

**🚀 Challenge:** Name the season you're in. Then ask God one question: "What's my assignment here?" Don't wait for a better season. Serve in this one.
