# SHORT 03 — Long Life Is God's Purpose
> Companion to Episode 3 · *Secrets of Longevity in 60 Seconds* · 30–60s

**Opening:** "Welcome to **Secrets of Longevity in 60 Seconds**."

**🎯 Hook:** God has never called anyone to retire from His purpose.

**📖 Scripture:** "...the number of thy days I will fulfil." — Exodus 23:26

**💡 Truth:** God didn't promise to fill your calendar. He promised to fulfill your days.
There's a difference. One is busyness. The other is a finished assignment.

**🚀 Challenge:** Live today like someone who still has an assignment — because you do.
