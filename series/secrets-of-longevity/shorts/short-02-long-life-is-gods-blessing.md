# SHORT 02 — Long Life Is God's Blessing
> Companion to Episode 2 · *Secrets of Longevity in 60 Seconds* · 30–60s

**Opening:** "Welcome to **Secrets of Longevity in 60 Seconds**."

**🎯 Hook:** Did you know the first promise of long life in the Bible wasn't about
medicine? It was about walking with God.

**📖 Scripture:** "With long life will I satisfy him, and shew him my salvation." —
Psalm 91:16

**💡 Truth:** God doesn't simply promise a *long* life. He promises a *satisfying* one. A
long life without purpose is empty. A short life with God has eternal value. But God's
desire is both — a long life that glorifies Him.

**🚀 Challenge:** Thank God today for the years He's already given — then ask Him to make
them fruitful.
