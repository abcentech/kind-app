# SHORT 04 — Feed Your Spirit
> Companion to Episode 4 · *Secrets of Longevity in 60 Seconds* · 30–60s

**Opening:** "Welcome to **Secrets of Longevity in 60 Seconds**."

**🎯 Hook:** Your stomach tells you when it's hungry. Your spirit can starve for months and never make a sound.

**📖 Scripture:** "Man shall not live by bread alone, but by every word that proceedeth out of the mouth of God." — Matthew 4:4

**💡 Truth:** What you feed grows. What you starve dies. You feed your body three times a day without thinking. When did you last feed the part of you that lives forever? The Word. Prayer. Worship. That's the menu.

**🚀 Challenge:** Tomorrow morning, feed your spirit before you touch your phone. Fifteen minutes. Word, prayer, worship — first.
