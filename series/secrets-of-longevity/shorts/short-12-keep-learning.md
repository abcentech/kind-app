# SHORT 12 — Keep Learning
> Companion to Episode 12 · *Secrets of Longevity in 60 Seconds* · 30–60s

**Opening:** "Welcome to **Secrets of Longevity in 60 Seconds**."

**🎯 Hook:** Your body stops growing in your teens — but your mind can keep growing until your
very last day. So why do so many people stop?

**📖 Scripture:** "A wise man will hear, and will increase learning." — Proverbs 1:5

**💡 Truth:** Moses was still learning from God at eighty. Daniel got fresh revelation in his
eighties. Paul asked for his books from death row. The wise never graduate. Every decade
should know more of God than the last.

**🚀 Challenge:** Study one verse today — don't just read it. Write down one new thing it
shows you about God.

---
**Quote graphic:** "A mind that stops learning becomes a fence post. A mind that keeps learning stays a tree."
