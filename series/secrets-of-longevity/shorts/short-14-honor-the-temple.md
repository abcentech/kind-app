# SHORT 14 — Honor the Temple
> Companion to Episode 14 · *Secrets of Longevity in 60 Seconds* · 30–60s

**Opening:** "Welcome to **Secrets of Longevity in 60 Seconds**."

**🎯 Hook:** What if the most sacred building on earth isn't made of stone — it's *you*?

**📖 Scripture:** "Your body is the temple of the Holy Ghost... ye are not your own." —
1 Corinthians 6:19–20

**💡 Truth:** God doesn't live in a building anymore. By His Spirit, He lives in you. You
were bought with the blood of Jesus. So your body isn't yours to ruin or to worship — it's
His to honor. Caring for it isn't vanity. It's worship.

**🚀 Challenge:** Today, do one thing for your body as an act of worship — and say, "Lord,
this is Yours."
