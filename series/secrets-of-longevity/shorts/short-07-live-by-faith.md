# SHORT 07 — Live by Faith
> Companion to Episode 7 · *Secrets of Longevity in 60 Seconds* · 30–60s

**Opening:** "Welcome to **Secrets of Longevity in 60 Seconds**."

**🎯 Hook:** At eighty-five, most people slow down. Caleb stood up and asked for a mountain still guarded by giants.

**📖 Scripture:** "Now the just shall live by faith: but if any man draw back, my soul shall have no pleasure in him." — Hebrews 10:38

**💡 Truth:** Anyone can believe for a moment. The crowd believed at the Red Sea and complained three days later. Faith isn't a single leap — it's the endurance that carries you across decades. Don't draw back. Keep climbing.

**🚀 Challenge:** Name the place you want to quit. Draw a line today: "I will not draw back." Then take one step forward.
