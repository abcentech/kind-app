# SHORT 21 — Remain Fruitful
> Companion to Episode 21 · *Secrets of Longevity in 60 Seconds* · 30–60s

**Opening:** "Welcome to **Secrets of Longevity in 60 Seconds**."

**🎯 Hook:** Did you know some olive trees are over a thousand years old — and still bearing
fruit? The oldest ones often make the richest oil.

**📖 Scripture:** "They shall still bring forth fruit in old age; they shall be fat and
flourishing." — Psalm 92:14

**💡 Truth:** The world says fruitfulness has a deadline. God says the righteous flourish
like the palm and grow like the cedar — taller, deeper, wider with age. Your last decades
are not your least. Stay rooted, keep abiding, keep bearing.

**🚀 Challenge:** Say it out loud today: "I shall still bring forth fruit." Then go give
away something only your years could have grown.
