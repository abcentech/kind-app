# SHORT 19 — Live One Day at a Time
> Companion to Episode 19 · *Secrets of Longevity in 60 Seconds* · 30–60s

**Opening:** "Welcome to **Secrets of Longevity in 60 Seconds**."

**🎯 Hook:** You will never have to live your whole life in a single day. So why are you
carrying it all at once?

**📖 Scripture:** "as thy days, so shall thy strength be." — Deuteronomy 33:25

**💡 Truth:** God doesn't give you tomorrow's strength today — because you don't need it
today. He gives manna fresh every morning. Worry empties today of the strength meant for
today. A faithful life is just a long string of obedient days.

**🚀 Challenge:** Hand God every tomorrow-worry. Then go do the one thing He's asking of you
*today*.

---
**Quote graphic:** "God gives strength by the day — and the day is always enough."
