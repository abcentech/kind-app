# SHORT 18 — Cleanse for Life
> Companion to Episode 18 · *Secrets of Longevity in 60 Seconds* · 30–60s

**Opening:** "Welcome to **Secrets of Longevity in 60 Seconds**."

**🎯 Hook:** What shortens most lives isn't one disaster — it's the small poisons we tolerate
every day.

**📖 Scripture:** "Let us cleanse ourselves from all filthiness of the flesh and spirit." —
2 Corinthians 7:1

**💡 Truth:** When Joseph was tempted, he didn't negotiate — he fled, leaving his coat
behind. He kept God's temple clean and lived a long, fruitful life. Temperance isn't your
willpower; it's the fruit of the Spirit. And what's defiled can be cleansed.

**🚀 Challenge:** Name one thing that defiles you — and flee it today. Then ask the Spirit
for self-control.
