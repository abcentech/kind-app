# SHORT 20 — Multiply Your Life
> Companion to Episode 20 · *Secrets of Longevity in 60 Seconds* · 30–60s

**Opening:** "Welcome to **Secrets of Longevity in 60 Seconds**."

**🎯 Hook:** Jesus never wrote a book or built a building. He poured into twelve men — and
it's still spreading across the earth today.

**📖 Scripture:** "the same commit thou to faithful men, who shall be able to teach others
also." — 2 Timothy 2:2

**💡 Truth:** You can count the seeds in an apple — but never the apples in a seed. Things
you own die with you. People you pour into carry you for generations. Stop trying to be
irreplaceable. Start being reproducible.

**🚀 Challenge:** Pick one person this week and pour into them what God poured into you.
