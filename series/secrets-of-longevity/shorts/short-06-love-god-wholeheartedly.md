# SHORT 06 — Love God Wholeheartedly
> Companion to Episode 6 · *Secrets of Longevity in 60 Seconds* · 30–60s

**Opening:** "Welcome to **Secrets of Longevity in 60 Seconds**."

**🎯 Hook:** Psalm 91 lists God's promises for thirteen verses. Then it reveals the one thing the man actually does to unlock them.

**📖 Scripture:** "Because he hath set his love upon me, therefore will I deliver him." — Psalm 91:14

**💡 Truth:** Not a ritual. Not a formula. He loved God. The long, satisfied life of Psalm 91 grows from one root — wholehearted love. You can do everything right and still miss the one thing. God wants your heart before your hands.

**🚀 Challenge:** Before you do one thing *for* God today, tell Him you love Him. Out loud. Relationship before religion.
