# SHORT 22 — Finish Your Race
> Companion to Episode 22 · *Secrets of Longevity in 60 Seconds* · 30–60s

**Opening:** "Welcome to **Secrets of Longevity in 60 Seconds**."

**🎯 Hook:** An Olympic runner once finished a marathon over an hour after the winner —
bleeding and limping. Why? "My country sent me to *finish* the race, not to start it."

**📖 Scripture:** "I have fought a good fight, I have finished my course, I have kept the
faith." — 2 Timothy 4:7

**💡 Truth:** Anyone can start. The blocks are crowded. Heaven doesn't celebrate the fastest
start — it celebrates the finish. Caleb finished with strength. Paul finished with joy. John
finished with confidence. The crown is for finishers.

**🚀 Challenge:** Don't coast and don't quit. Ask God for a fresh mountain — and run
*through* the line.
