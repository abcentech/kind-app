# SHORT 17 — Move for Life
> Companion to Episode 17 · *Secrets of Longevity in 60 Seconds* · 30–60s

**Opening:** "Welcome to **Secrets of Longevity in 60 Seconds**."

**🎯 Hook:** At eighty-five, Caleb didn't ask for a recliner. He said, "Give me this
mountain."

**📖 Scripture:** "The LORD God... put the man into the garden of Eden to dress it and to
keep it." — Genesis 2:15

**💡 Truth:** God's first gift to man, after breath, was work — a body made to move. A hinge
that's never used rusts shut. Movement keeps the temple strong and ready to serve. Bodily
exercise profits — kept in its place, under godliness.

**🚀 Challenge:** Take a walk today. Use it to pray, thank God for your body, and keep the
hinge swinging.
