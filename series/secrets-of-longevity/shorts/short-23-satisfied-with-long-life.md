# SHORT 23 — Satisfied with Long Life
> Companion to Episode 23 · *Secrets of Longevity in 60 Seconds* · 30–60s

**Opening:** "Welcome to **Secrets of Longevity in 60 Seconds**."

**🎯 Hook:** People at the very end of life almost never regret owning less or working less.
The deepest regrets are always about what truly mattered.

**📖 Scripture:** "With long life will I satisfy him, and shew him my salvation." —
Psalm 91:16

**💡 Truth:** God's promise was never just *more years.* It was *satisfaction* — a life that
arrives full. The verse doesn't end with "long life." It ends with "and shew him my
salvation." Old Simeon could die in peace, not because he lived long, but because his eyes
had seen the Savior.

**🚀 Challenge:** Make sure of Christ today. Live this day so that at the end you hear, "Well
done, good and faithful servant."

---
**Quote graphic:** "The longest life is the one that ends able to say: 'It was worth every season — and I have seen the Lord.'"
